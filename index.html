Design Principles

Good web design follows several key principles that make content easy to read and navigate. This page incorporates:

Readability: Clear fonts, adequate line spacing, and good contrast between text and background colors ensure the content is easy to read.

Visual Hierarchy: Different heading sizes, colors, and spacing help organize information and guide the reader's attention.

Consistency: Consistent styling throughout the page creates a cohesive and professional appearance.
